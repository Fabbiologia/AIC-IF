How It Works

For Researchers

  1. Your papers, datasets, and code are registered with unique identifiers
  2. When AI systems use your work, the citation is automatically logged
  3. SHAP/LIME analysis quantifies the importance of your contribution
  4. You receive credit in real-time as your work influences AI outputs
  5. Your AIC-IF score updates dynamically based on actual usage

For AI Developers

  1. Integrate with the AIC-IF API to log citations automatically
  2. Improve transparency by attributing sources properly
  3. Use the feature contribution analysis to understand model behavior
  4. Build trust by ensuring proper attribution of scientific content
  5. Access the knowledge graph to enrich your AI's understanding
